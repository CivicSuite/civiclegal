CivicSuite / CivicLegal

Legal record support without automating legal judgment.

CivicLegal v0.1.2 helps authorized municipal legal staff search city records with citations, look up prior actions, draft attorney-reviewed memo scaffolds, persist optional memo/hold workpapers, compare ordinance drafts, and flag litigation-hold candidates.

Ships Today

Deterministic privilege-aware filtering now reuses the shared CivicCore search-access helpers, cited lookup helpers, drafting scaffolds, optional persistence with CIVICLEGAL_WORKPAPER_DB_URL, tests, release gate, and browser QA evidence.

Attorney Control

City attorneys control corpus tiers, review drafts, and make every legal judgment.

Boundary

No legal advice, Westlaw/Lexis replacement, autonomous legal conclusions, court filing, live LLM calls, or legal-system connector runtime ships in v0.1.2.
